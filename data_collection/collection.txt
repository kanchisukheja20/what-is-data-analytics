 data collection is a process of collecting structured ,unstructured 
,uncleaned or cleaned information from multiple platform example kaggle etc.
It means gathering raw data from different sources for analysis.
It is the first step of any data-related work, and the quality of analysis depends heavily on how the data is collected.

The purpose of data collection is to ensure that the data is relevant, reliable, and sufficient for answering a question or solving a problem.

Main uses

Understanding situations or trends

Supporting analysis and reporting

Making business or operational decisions

Common tools & sources

Surveys & forms – primary data from users or customers

Databases (SQL) – structured organizational data

Excel / CSV files – stored or shared data

APIs & web data – automated data extraction

Internal systems – ERP, CRM, finance, sales systems

In simple terms: collect the right data first, then clean and analyze it.
