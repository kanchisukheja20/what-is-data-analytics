Data forecasting means using past data to predict future outcomes.
It focuses on identifying patterns and trends in historical data to estimate what is likely to happen next.

The purpose of data forecasting is to support planning and decision-making by reducing uncertainty about the future.

Main uses

Sales and demand planning

Budgeting and financial planning

Inventory and resource management

Risk assessment

Common tools

Excel – simple trend and forecast functions

Python – statistical and time-series models

SQL – preparing historical data

Power BI / Tableau – displaying forecast results
